Example of iframe DOM movement resetting the iframe

To demonstrate the issue:

  1. Type something both into the text area and into the search box within the iframe
  2. Press the "Swap content location" button
  3. Observe that the iframe will visibly blink to reset, and any local state (e.g., text within the search box) will be lost. By contrast, the non-iframe text area will keep its state.
